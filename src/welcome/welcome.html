No More Slop

You're in.

The slop is off. Click the extension in your toolbar — or press Alt+Shift+S — to set your first intent.

How it works

  1. Set an intent. What are you here to do? Type it. Or click Just block slop for no-task mode.
  2. Pick what to block. YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, Snapchat, X, Reddit. All on by default.
  3. Get back to work. Try to slip into a feed and you'll get a toast reminding you why you're here.

Features worth knowing

  • Strict mode — locks toggles and requires typing "end" to finish. No quick bypasses.
  • Break mode — need a breather? Pick 5/10/15/30 min and get everything back for that window.
  • Dark mode — the sun icon, top-right of the popup.
  • Session summary — when you end a session, see duration and blocks caught.

Support the project

No More Slop is free and open source. If it's giving you your time back, consider leaving a tip.
